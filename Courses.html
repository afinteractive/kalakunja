Courses

Unlock Your Musical Talent with Comprehensive Courses Tailored for Every Instrument and Style

Vocal (Classical, Modern, Folk, Ghazals)

Our vocal course is designed to cater to diverse musical preferences and skill levels. Whether you are interested in the intricacies of classical music, the versatility and innovation of modern genres, the rich cultural heritage of folk music, or the soulful expression of ghazals, our experienced instructors will guide you every step of the way. With a focus on voice training, breathing techniques, and performance, we help students develop their unique vocal style while mastering both traditional and contemporary forms of singing.

Tabla (Classical, Light including Madal, Naal, Dholak, Mridangam)

The tabla course offers an in-depth exploration of Indian percussion, blending traditional and modern styles. Students will start by learning the fundamentals of rhythm and hand techniques, progressing to complex compositions and improvisations. The course also includes lessons on related instruments such as the Madal, Naal, Dholak, and Mridangam, giving students a comprehensive understanding of the different rhythms in classical and light music. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced player, you will gain a strong command of rhythmic cycles and improvisational skills.

Violin (Classical, Eastern, and Western)

Our violin course offers a balanced focus on classical, Eastern, and Western music traditions, making it perfect for students looking to expand their versatility as a violinist. Students will learn proper posture, bowing techniques, and finger positioning, ensuring a strong technical foundation. Whether you want to perform classical pieces, engage with Eastern traditions, or explore Western orchestral and contemporary music, our instructors will tailor the lessons to suit your goals, helping you develop as a well-rounded violinist.

Guitar (Classical, Eastern, Western, Jazz, Metal, Blues, Pop, Reggae)

The guitar course covers a broad range of genres, offering something for every aspiring guitarist. Whether you're drawn to the timeless elegance of classical guitar, the intricate scales of Eastern music, or the expressive energy of Western styles like jazz, rock, and metal, our curriculum is designed to challenge and inspire. You’ll explore techniques such as fingerstyle, power chords, and soloing, while diving deep into genre-specific elements like jazz improvisation, blues phrasing, and metal shredding. Our goal is to help you master the guitar and find your voice in any style you choose to pursue.

Piano/Keyboard

In our piano and keyboard course, students will learn everything from basic music theory and finger techniques to advanced compositions and improvisations. The course covers classical repertoire, contemporary pop songs, and even electronic music production, depending on the student’s interests. With a focus on both performance and musical interpretation, this course will help you build strong technical skills while encouraging creative expression. Whether you're preparing for a recital or just playing for fun, you'll develop a strong foundation in keyboard instruments.

Drums/Congo

Our drums and congo course is designed for those who want to explore the world of percussion. The course covers fundamental techniques such as stick control, coordination, and rhythm reading, as well as more advanced patterns, fills, and solos. You will also learn different drumming styles across various genres like rock, jazz, and Latin music. The hands-on approach ensures that you develop a solid sense of rhythm and timing, essential for both solo performances and group settings. Whether you aim to play in a band or simply enjoy the power of rhythm, this course has something for every level.

Harmonium, Flute, Sarangi

Our harmonium, flute, and sarangi course dives deep into the rich tradition of Indian classical music while also introducing students to contemporary applications of these instruments. You’ll start by learning proper fingering and breath control (for flute), and progress to more advanced ragas and compositions. Whether your interest lies in solo performance or accompanying other musicians, this course provides a holistic understanding of each instrument. Our instructors will guide you through techniques that enhance tone, phrasing, and expression, making these traditional instruments come alive in your hands.
